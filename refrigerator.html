Common Issues

Refrigerator Problems We Fix

Our technicians are trained to handle every type of refrigerator issue, big or small.

Not Cooling Properly

Warm interior, inconsistent temperatures, or food spoiling quickly. We diagnose thermostat, fan, and airflow issues.

Water Leaking

Puddles under or inside the fridge. Often caused by clogged defrost drains, broken water lines, or faulty inlet valves.

Ice Maker Malfunction

No ice production, small or misshapen cubes, or ice maker leaking. We repair and replace ice maker assemblies.

Compressor Failure

Clicking sounds, fridge running constantly, or not starting at all. We repair and replace compressors for all brands.

Frost Buildup

Excessive ice or frost in freezer or fridge compartments. Usually caused by defrost system failures or door seal issues.

Unusual Noises

Buzzing, humming, clicking, or rattling sounds. We identify whether it's a fan motor, compressor, or condenser issue.

Door Seal Problems

Worn, torn, or loose gaskets causing warm air leaks. We replace door seals to restore proper insulation and efficiency.

Control Board Issues

Display errors, unresponsive controls, or erratic temperature. We diagnose and replace faulty electronic control boards.

Answers to common questions about our refrigerator repair services.

Refrigerator repair costs typically range from $150 to $400 depending on the issue. Simple fixes like thermostat replacements are on the lower end, while compressor repairs are higher. We always provide an upfront quote before starting work, so there are no surprises.

Generally, if the repair cost is less than 50% of a new refrigerator's price and it's under 15 years old, repair is the better option. Our technicians will give you honest advice about whether repair or replacement makes more sense for your situation.
